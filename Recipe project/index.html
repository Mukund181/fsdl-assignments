My Favourite Healthy Recipes 😋


Egg Bhurji
  1. Whisk eggs with salt and turmeric.
  2. Sauté onions until translucent.
  3. Add tomatoes and green chillies.
  4. Add chilli powder and garam masala.
  5. Pour eggs and stir until set.
  6. Garnish with coriander and lemon.

Grilled Chicken
  1. Flatten chicken breasts evenly.
  2. Marinate with olive oil, lemon and garlic.
  3. Chill for 20 minutes.
  4. Grill 6 minutes each side.
  5. Rest 5 minutes before slicing.

Overnight Oats
  1. Mix oats and milk.
  2. Add protein powder.
  3. Add chia seeds and yogurt.
  4. Sweeten lightly.
  5. Refrigerate for 4 hours.
  6. Top with nuts before serving.

Palak Paneer
  1. Blanch spinach.
  2. Make spinach puree.
  3. Sauté cumin, garlic and onion.
  4. Add spices.
  5. Add puree and simmer.
  6. Fold in paneer cubes.
